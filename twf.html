Marketing Contractor

Marketing Contractor/Internship

The Wealthy Franchise works with a group of people that are interested in opening up franchise locations across Canada. We work with companies that have a strong brand, a good system in place, and have a product or service that is scalable. We offer commissions and hourly wages depending on the contracts you secure.

Job Description


We are also looking to hire someone who can find buyers for our clients (which includes a fortune 500 company.) We have clients in the accounting, nutrition, law, coffee, tutoring and food industry. The average commission you would receive would be $10,000 for a sale. Your main job would be to advertise online, go to networking events, send emails, and spread the word that our clients are looking to open locations across Canada. If you can send us a lead that buys, you get your commission.
